## Key Caveats

1. **UP2 values are computed from MapPLUTO polygons**, not the game's internal data (public API was 503). They're close but not exact. A structure that "barely fits" by our numbers might not fit in-game, or vice versa.

2. **Structure min sizes are from community research**, not official Upland docs. Residential sizes are well-tested; service structure sizes are estimated from relative categories. Always test in the Playground.

3. **Multi-structure properties**: Some properties already have multiple structures (e.g., Bodega + Bus Stop). When demolishing, you may lose all structures on that property. Plan accordingly.

4. **20 demolitions** is aggressive. You can phase this — do the highest-impact demolitions first (Phase 1 = 10 structures) and see how the Resident Score responds before proceeding.

5. **Spark Hours** are the real bottleneck. Prioritize the structures with the highest SU-per-SparkHour ratio.
